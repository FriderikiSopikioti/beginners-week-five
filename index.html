Lizards

The geckos, the iguanas and the skinks are like little dinosuars in a way, especially the iguanas. There is a fair chance you will see them during your visit in the Caribbean islands, and you will learn more about these wonderful creatures.More info on Lizards here

Dolphins

There are opportunities for people to see dolphins from a close distance, in sheltered areas and there are also many special dolphin encounters for young children. The most interesting experience though is seeing dolphins in their natural habitat on a dolphin spotting trip. More info on Dolphins here.

Turtles

Caribbean tutles often can be seen nesting on protected beaches where several turtle conservation projects are in hand.More info on turtles here.

Birds

The Caribbean islands are known to be the home of rare kinds of birds and that is why the activity of birdwatching is very popular there. Birding enthusiasts can discover hundreds of species of birds and ornithologists find the Caribbean one of the most rewarding regions to go on a birdwatching holiday.More info on birds here.

Tigers Sharks

The illustrious tiger shark is a circumglobal species that can be found in a variety of places, one of which is the Caribbean sea.More info on Tiger sharks here.

Whales

For most of the year whales can be spotted somewhere in the Caribbean. The months of summer are the best for spotting migrating pilot whales at the Bahamas.More info on whales here.
